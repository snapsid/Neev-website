WHAT IS NEEV ?

Neev - The whirlwind of tech, is an initiative from the Dot QuestionMark which introduces a series of 9 distinct events, all that promises to build a foundation for the knowledge of any specific domain.

Duration

2 months program with 100 + hours.

Projects

Enhance your learning and CV simaltaneously.

Mentor Support

The 24 hours assistance, whenever you need it.

Certification

Training and project Certificate by Dot Questionmark.

NO Cost

Discover free online learning from best online courses by covering different domains.

Eligibility

Indian Students pursuing any technical degree namely B.Tech, BE, MCA, BCA, BSc IT (*from any stream).

QUIZ NIGHT

This is one of the most fun nights of the week where we pick the topic of the day and conduct a fun live quiz session. It helps you experiment and learn different technologies. This quiz is in the Multiple Choice Questions based format which is held every Monday.

PROJECT ARENA

We all know after whatever we learn from theories and practicals, it all comes to building a Project to visualize and understand the actual implementation of the technology. We conduct this Project Arena session every weekend i.e., Saturdays and Sundays, where we aim to pick a project from every possible domain and keep it in front of our audience. So attend these sessions and it will be a plus one on your resume.

TECH TALK

We all have a confusion in our mind related to technology implementation in industry. In this segment we aim to provide Mentorship directly from the Industry Experts.
